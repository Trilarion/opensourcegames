Block-based 3D space crafting sandbox: explore procedurally generated planets across many star systems, mine and craft, tame creatures, build ships, bases and space stations block by block — solo or on self-hostable multiplayer servers. Unity client with an authoritative .NET server, system-scale space flight and an optional AI-driven story campaign.
